About

Trail length: 8.7 miles

Trail description: The Lower Las Vegas Wash Trail is an urban asphalt trail that offers scenic views. Dogs are able to use this trail but must be kept on a leash.

Skill level: All skill levels

Parking: Parking for the Lower Las Vegas Wash Trail is available in North Las Vegas at Sandstone Ridge Park (1661 W. Hammer Lane) and Craig Ranch Regional Park (628 W. Craig Road), as well as at trailheads off Coleman Street, W. Washburn Road and E. Cheyenne Avenue.
