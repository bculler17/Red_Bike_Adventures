HOW IT WORKS

1. Unlock

Pick up a bike at one of hundreds of Red Bike Adventures stations. Stations can easily be found by using the SYSTEM MAP. Use the self pay kiosk at the station to unlock the bike. Pricing options can be found HERE.

2. Ride

Adventure it up for the amount of time you reserved. Reservation options can be found HERE. If you exceed your time, no worries! Your reservation will be automatically extended and your card on file will be charged $8 to upgrade to an All-Day Adventure. After 24 hours is exceeded, your card on file will be charged $10 a day until the bike is returned.

3. Return

Your adventure ends by returning your bike to any Red Bike Aventures station. Push your bike firmly into an empty dock and wait for the green light to make sure it's locked.
