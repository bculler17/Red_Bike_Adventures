Little Red Rock Trail

About

Trail length: 7 miles

Trail description: The Little Red Rock Trail is a dirt mountain trail that winds through scenic red rock and offers beautiful views of the valley in Red Rock Canyon. Dogs are welcome. The Little Red Rock Trail is one of the most visited biking trails in Nevada, with roughly one million visitors each year, due to its fun ride and amazing views.

Skill level: Intermediate

Parking: 187 Rakkasan Way, Las Vegas, NV 89161
